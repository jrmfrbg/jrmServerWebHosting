Project Overview

This interactive platform explores the fundamental structural differences between the three main classes of plastics. By simulating molecular chains and their cross-links, we visualize how macroscopic properties like elasticity and thermal stability emerge from microscopic interactions.

Class I

Elastomers

Weakly cross-linked chains that exhibit entropic recovery. Explore how random coils enable high elasticity.

Launch Sim
Class II

Thermoplasts

Linear chains held by secondary bonds. Observe the transition between glassy and molten states.

Launch Sim
Class III

Duroplasts

Dense 3D covalent networks. Discover why these materials remain rigid even at high temperatures.
